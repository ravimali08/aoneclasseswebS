About AONE Classes

Building English speaking confidence and academic excellence in Anjar since inception. Discover our history, teaching methods, and core values.

Our Identity

A Premium Spoken English & Commerce Coaching Institute

AONE Classes Anjar is not a traditional school or online platform. We are a specialized offline Spoken English and Commerce coaching center dedicated to helping Gujarati-medium and Hindi-medium students succeed in today's English-speaking world.

Our Spoken English curriculum is built around communication confidence. We help students bridge the gap between understanding English and speaking it fluently in daily life, office environments, and public spaces.

Additionally, we run highly successful Commerce board preps and degree guides, assisting students in scoring top results in their academic journeys.

Why Students Trust Us

  • Personalized feedback for every student
  • Special emphasis on eliminating public speaking anxiety
  • Small interactive batch structures
  • Local Gujarati language references to explain grammar rules

Our Mission

To empower Gujarati-medium and Hindi-medium students to speak English confidently, remove their fear of communication, and open doors to global academic, career, and business opportunities.

Our Vision

To build a modern offline education hub in Anjar where students learn English not by reading notebooks, but by active communication, discussion, and practical exposure in an interactive workspace.

Philosophy

Learn by Speaking, Not Just Studying

Traditional classrooms force students to write down complex rules, leading to excellent grammar scores but low spoken confidence. Here is how our philosophy is different:

1. Immediate Participation

Students start speaking simple sentences from day one. Communication overcomes fear before detailed mechanics are taught.

2. Activity Integration

Vocabulary and idioms are picked up naturally through storybooks, presentation prep, movie discussions, and debates.

3. Real-Life Simulation

Roleplay activities mirror exact settings like ordering at cafes, answering managers, or presenting project pitches.
